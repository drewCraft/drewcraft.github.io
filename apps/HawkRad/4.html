Conclusion

Hawking’s Theory of black hole decay, by applying knowledge of Virtual Particles and the effects of an Event Horizon, show that black holes emit Hawking Radiation. Although the concept itself is relatively simple compared to the mountain of knowledge required to understand it, this theory’s importance is still seen today. In fact new evidence may bring this theory into reality. Scientists have run experiments with artificial even horizons, looking for hawking radiation emission, and they think they saw some (ArXiv, 2012). Perhaps, his theory will be proven incorrect by another brilliant scientist, as unafraid of progress as Hawking. Regardless, Stephen Hawking himself never believed in one grand Theory of Everything, yet he would undoubtedly be pleased that his theory enacted as a stepping stone for the future to unveil itself onto the world.

I hope these simulations helped spread this knowledge to others in a someone function and approachable way.
