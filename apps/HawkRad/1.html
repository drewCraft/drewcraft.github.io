Simulation 1

Event Horizons

Event horizons describe the radius of a black hole, past which, nothing can escape its gravitational influence.

In this simulation, although objects are not to scale, all occurs in a very short period of distance and time. Click anywhere inside the canvas to create a proton. you will see that as the protons are pulled closer to the event horizon, they pick up speed, until they pass the threshold, from which they will never return.
