Introduction

So what is Hawking Radiation, and how does it affect the scientific world?

Hawking Radiation is the result of interference between General Relativity and Quantum Theory. General Relativity outlines gravity as curves in space-time rather than a force, and Quantum Theory describes everything as being made of discrete and very small units called quanta. These two very important theories are not very compatible, interacting in very problematic ways.

Out of every conflict between the two models, black holes, in a literal sense, are the most massive. The very concept of an event horizon appears contradict the laws of Thermodynamics and Symmetry, yet these issues were nearly completely resolved by the late Stephen Hawking and his theory of black hole decay.
